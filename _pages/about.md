I'm Omprakash Jena, a Generative AI Solutions Architect and Consultant based in the UK, working with Tata Consultancy Services.

My career spans the full data and AI spectrum — from Data Science and Machine Learning, through Business Intelligence, Data Engineering, and Conversational AI, to where I am today: designing enterprise-grade GenAI solutions. That breadth means I bring genuine end-to-end perspective, not just a narrow specialisation.

I architect complete solutions — from AI pipelines on **Databricks** and **Azure OpenAI**, built with frameworks like **LangChain**, **LangGraph**, and **MLflow**, to **Python FastAPI** backends, **PostgreSQL** databases, and **React** frontends — all aligned to enterprise **microservices and microfrontend** architecture patterns. I also produce the full suite of design artefacts: solution blueprints, **Architecture & Information Assurance (AIA)** documents, and low-level designs.

I stay hands-on too — building PoCs and prototypes that validate innovative ideas before they scale into production.

Across industries including **Manufacturing, Utilities, Supply Chain, Travel, and ITSM**, I've worked at the intersection of technical depth and business impact — translating complex AI capabilities into solutions that actually move the needle.
